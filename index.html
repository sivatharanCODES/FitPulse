How it works

Your daily dose of fitness in 3 simple steps

01

Track and personalize your profile

FitPulse starts by analyzing your fitness data to understand your body better. The app records your weight progress, percentage of exercise completed, blood hemoglobin (Hg) levels, and preferred workout types. You can select your favorite exercises or training styles, and FitPulse AI will build a personalized plan that fits your body and goals perfectly.

02

Monitor and schedule your activities

FitPulse automatically tracks your daily activity schedule with detailed date logs and heart rate monitoring. You can view your progress on an interactive timeline, adjust training intensity, or even add new custom activities at any time. The app keeps your fitness journey organized and goal-driven every single day.

03

Train smart with live feedback

Once you start your workout, FitPulse continuously monitors your performance in real time. You’ll receive instant feedback, posture corrections, and smart suggestions to help you improve form, endurance, and recovery. It’s like having a professional trainer watching and guiding you every step of the way.
